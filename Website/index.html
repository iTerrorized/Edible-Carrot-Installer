Getting Started

  1. Click Add to VCC below to add this package listing
  2. In VCC, add Edible Carrot System to your avatar project
  3. In Unity, add the Edible Carrot Installer component to your avatar
  4. Enter your license key and click Verify
  5. Choose your hand mode, click Start Setup, position, then Finish Setup
